Pottery Paw is a place that
opens up new masters

The journey to becoming a ceramic master is one of dedication, patience, and continuous learning. It often begins with an apprenticeship under the guidance of a seasoned artisan, where the nuances of the craft are learned through hands-on experience.
Pottery Paw artisans possess a deep understanding of the properties of their materials, the intricacies of glazing, and the alchemy of the kiln.
